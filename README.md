## Privacy Policy

ASIANA is committed to protecting your privacy.

* **Location Data:** We collect precise location data to provide real-time navigation for drivers and delivery tracking for dispatchers. This data is only collected while the app is in use or in the background during an active delivery.

* **Account Info:** We use Supabase for secure authentication. We do not share your personal information with third parties.

* **Data Security:** All data is encrypted and stored securely.
